penggunaan JOIN :

Tabel users 
+----+----------+----------------------------+
| id | name     | email                      |

Tabel barang
+----+----------+-------------+-------+
| id | users_id | nama_produk | harga |
+----+----------+-------------+-------+
|  1 |        2 | Keyboard    | 50000 |
|  3 |        2 | Headset     | 70000 |
|  4 |        3 | Mouse       | 15000 |
+----+----------+-------------+-------+


query join 
SELECT users.name, users.email, barang.nama_produk, barang.harga FROM users INNER JOIN barang ON users.id = barang.users_id;
